This is an unofficial companion app for the board game Sleeping Gods by RedRavenGames.

It can be used to track your progress in the game. If this app suits your needs, feel free to use it.

Not compatible with any expansions to the game. Internationalization is not provided or planned.

There is no tracking, and no cookies are being used. Anything you enter will be stored in your browser and never leave your device.
